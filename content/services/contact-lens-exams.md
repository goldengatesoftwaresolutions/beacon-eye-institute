## Contact Lens Exams

Not all contact lenses are the same, and finding the right fit is crucial for comfortable, clear vision and eye health. Our optometrists perform specialized contact lens exams to find the perfect lenses for your eyes.

### Types of Contact Lenses We Fit

- **Soft contact lenses** — Daily, bi-weekly, and monthly disposable lenses
- **Toric lenses** — Specially designed for patients with astigmatism
- **Multifocal lenses** — For patients who need both distance and near correction
- **Rigid gas-permeable (RGP) lenses** — Provide sharp vision for patients with irregular corneas
- **Scleral lenses** — Large-diameter lenses ideal for keratoconus and severe dry eye
- **Specialty lenses** — Orthokeratology (overnight lenses) and more

### What to Expect at Your Fitting

During your contact lens exam, we will:
1. Measure the curvature and size of your eyes
2. Evaluate your tear film and eye health
3. Determine your contact lens prescription
4. Trial fit lenses and assess comfort and vision
5. Provide instructions on care and handling
