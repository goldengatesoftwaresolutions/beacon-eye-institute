## Dry Eye Treatment

Dry eye is a common condition in which your eyes don't produce enough tears, or the tears they produce evaporate too quickly. It can cause significant discomfort and affect your quality of life.

### Symptoms of Dry Eye

- Stinging, burning, or scratchy sensation in your eyes
- Stringy mucus in or around your eyes
- Sensitivity to light
- Eye redness
- Feeling like something is in your eyes
- Difficulty wearing contact lenses
- Blurred vision or eye fatigue

### Treatment Options

Our team offers a comprehensive approach to dry eye treatment:

- **Artificial tears** — Over-the-counter lubricating drops for mild symptoms
- **Prescription eye drops** — Cyclosporine (Restasis) or lifitegrast (Xiidra) to reduce inflammation
- **Punctal plugs** — Tiny devices inserted into the tear ducts to retain moisture
- **LipiFlow** — Thermal pulsation treatment to unblock Meibomian glands
- **Lifestyle modifications** — Dietary changes, humidifiers, and screen break habits
