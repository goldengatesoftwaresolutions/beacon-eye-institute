## Comprehensive Eye Exams

Regular eye exams are essential for maintaining your vision health and overall well-being. Our optometrists perform thorough eye examinations to assess your visual acuity, check for refractive errors, and evaluate the health of your eyes.

### What to Expect

During a comprehensive eye exam, we will:
- Test your visual acuity at near and far distances
- Check for nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism
- Measure eye pressure to screen for glaucoma
- Examine the front and back of your eyes
- Dilate your pupils to evaluate the optic nerve and retina

### Contact Lens Exams

If you wear or want to wear contact lenses, we offer specialized contact lens exams to determine the right prescription and lens type for your eyes.

### How Often Should You Have an Eye Exam?

We recommend annual eye exams for most adults. Patients with diabetes, glaucoma, or other risk factors may need more frequent visits.
