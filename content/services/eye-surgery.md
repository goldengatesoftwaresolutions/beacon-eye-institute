## Eye Surgery at Beacon Eye Institute

Dr. Ngoc Nguyen, M.D. is a board-certified ophthalmologist with extensive surgical training and experience. Our practice offers a range of ophthalmic surgical procedures to treat vision-threatening conditions and improve quality of life.

### Surgical Services We Offer

#### Cataract Surgery
Removal of the clouded natural lens and replacement with a clear intraocular lens (IOL). One of the safest and most commonly performed surgeries in the world.
[Learn more about Cataract Surgery](/services/cataract-surgery/)

#### Glaucoma Surgery
When eye drops or laser therapy are insufficient, surgical options such as trabeculectomy and drainage device implantation can effectively lower intraocular pressure and protect the optic nerve.
[Learn more about Glaucoma Treatment](/services/glaucoma-treatment/)

#### Corneal Cross-Linking
A minimally invasive procedure to strengthen the cornea and halt the progression of keratoconus.

### What to Expect

Dr. Nguyen takes a thorough, patient-centered approach to surgical care. Every procedure begins with a comprehensive evaluation to confirm candidacy and discuss goals, risks, and expected outcomes. Most procedures are performed on an outpatient basis, and our team provides detailed aftercare instructions to support a smooth recovery.
